Experience Night City Like Never Before

From the rough streets of Watson to the sleek luxury of City Center, our expert guides will show you the city through a unique lens - its (cash) rich history, its dangerous underbelly, and its mind-blowing tech. Explore with us and dive deep into the pulse of a city that never sleeps. But before you embark on this thrilling adventure, we recommend to read about the different districts and don't forget to check out our gallery of NC to get you familiar with the environment beforehand

South Watson: The Heart of the Hustle

Explore the rough, neon-drenched streets of South Watson, a district where the pulse of Night City beats strongest. Here, you'll see the fusion of old and new-crumbling industrial buildings and slick, towering complexes side by side. South Watson is home to the constant hustle of street vendors, cyber-enhanced mercenaries, and the daily struggles of the working class. Get up close with the notorious Maelstrom gang, witness street racing in action, and explore the district's dark alleys, where danger and opportunity lurk around every corner. It's a place of raw energy and hidden stories waiting to be uncovered.

City Center: The Corporate Dreamscape

The glittering heart of Night City, City Center is a world of excess, wealth, and corporate power. As you glide through towering skyscrapers and glass-covered mega-buildings, you’ll be surrounded by the sleek, high-tech side of the city—the shiny surface hiding a web of intrigue beneath. From the massive corporate headquarters to luxurious shopping districts, City Center is where the elite live, work, and play. But even here, in the shadows of glass and steel, you’ll find corporate espionage, dirty secrets, and the merciless pursuit of profit. Take in panoramic views from the top floors, or explore the streets below where power is bought and sold.

Heywood: The Heartbeat of the People

A vibrant, densely populated district, Heywood is where Night City's spirit truly comes alive. As you stroll through its colorful streets, you’ll experience the mix of cultures, music, and community that defines this neighborhood. Street art covers the walls, neon signs flicker overhead, and local markets hum with activity. Heywood is home to a variety of factions, from the humble to the dangerous—turf wars, local gangs, and family-run businesses make up the fabric of this district. The scent of street food fills the air, while the sounds of music and chatter create a lively atmosphere that never fades. It’s the pulse of Night City’s working class, and it’s a place where stories are born.

North Pacifica: The Fallen Paradise

Once envisioned as the crown jewel of Night City, North Pacifica is now a dystopian playground of abandoned luxury and desperate survival. Formerly home to luxury resorts and high-end living, the district now lies in decay, overtaken by violent gangs and corporate interests. As you journey through its decaying streets, you'll witness the remnants of what was supposed to be a utopia—now a city of ruins, broken dreams, and dangerous turf wars. From the beachfront mansions now controlled by gangs to the eerie silence of abandoned tourist spots, North Pacifica is a haunting reflection of the highs and lows of Night City. It's a place where the wealthy once lived and the desperate now struggle for survival.

Dogtown: Night City's Wild West

Dogtown is the lawless heart of Night City, a district where chaos reigns and survival is a daily battle. Once a thriving area, it’s now a sprawling, anarchic zone filled with rogue gangs, street vendors, and black-market tech deals. The streets are alive with graffiti, the air thick with the hum of illegal activity, and danger lurks around every corner. Here, freedom is both a blessing and a curse, as those who live in Doge Town carve out their existence outside the corporate grip. If you're looking for raw, unfiltered Night City, this is where you'll find it.
